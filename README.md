Superstore Power BI Dashboard

This repository contains an interactive **Power BI dashboard** analyzing sales, profit, and product performance using the Superstore dataset.  
This project demonstrates skills in data modeling, DAX, visualization design, and business analytics.

---

 Key Features

**1. KPI Metrics**
- Total Sales  
- Total Quantity  
- Total Profit  
- Profit Margin  

**2. Monthly Sales Trend**
Line chart showing seasonality and month-to-month sales performance.

**3. Sales by Category**
Breakdown of sales across:
- Technology  
- Furniture  
- Office Supplies  

**4. Profit by Category**
Highlights which product categories drive the most profit.

**5. Sales by Sub-Category**
Horizontal bar chart showing the strongest and weakest performing sub-categories.

**6. Sales by State (Shape Map)**
Geographic visualization illustrating U.S. sales distribution by state.

 **7. Top 10 Products by Sales**
Bar chart ranking the highest-revenue products.
